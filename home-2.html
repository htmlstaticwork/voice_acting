Studio Standard Guidelines

To maintain the highest quality for our clients, we expect the following standards from our rostered talent.

Hardware & Tech

  • 1

    XLR Microphone

    Professional condenser microphone required. No USB mics for premium tier.

  • 2

    Audio Interface

    Dedicated interface (Focusrite, Apollo, etc.) with phantom power.

  • 3

    Acoustic Treatment

    Studio booth or treated room with -60dB noise floor or better.

File Specifications

  • 4

    Sampling Rate

    Minimum 48kHz / 24-bit WAV format. No MP3 masters.

  • 5

    Clean Raw Files

    Deliver raw tracks with -3dB peak and -18dB RMS levels.

  • 6

    Turnaround Time

    Standard 24-hour turnaround for most commercial projects.
