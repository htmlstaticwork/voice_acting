Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard turnaround time?
Most commercial projects are delivered within 24 hours. For longer audiobooks or complex video games, we will provide a custom timeline.
Do I own the rights to the audio?
Yes, standard buyout for digital use is included in our base prices. Enterprise and broadcast usage rights can be negotiated during the hiring process.
